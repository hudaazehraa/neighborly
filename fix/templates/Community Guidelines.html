Community Guidelines

Our goal is to keep Neighborly a safe, respectful, and welcoming place for everyone.

What You Should Do

  • Be Respectful: Treat others the way you want to be treated.
  • Stay Relevant: Keep discussions focused on community topics.
  • Use Clear Language: Write in a way that’s easy for others to understand.
  • Report Issues: If you see something harmful, report it instead of responding negatively.
  • Protect Privacy: Share your own information carefully and respect the privacy of others.

What You Shouldn’t Do

  • No Harassment or Bullying: Do not insult, threaten, or target anyone.
  • No Hate Speech or Discrimination: Attacks based on race, religion, gender, or identity will not be tolerated.
  • No Spam or Self-Promotion: Don’t flood the community with ads or irrelevant links.
  • No Illegal Content: Don’t share anything that violates the law.
  • No Misinformation: Avoid spreading rumors, false or misleading content.

Consequences

Breaking these rules may lead to warnings, content removal, or suspension of your account.

Our Goal

We’re building a supportive, helpful, and friendly community. Thank you for keeping Neighborly a safe place for everyone!
